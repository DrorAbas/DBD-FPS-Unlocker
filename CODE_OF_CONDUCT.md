# Code of Conduct

Be respectful and constructive.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, spam and intentionally disruptive behavior are not welcome.

When reporting a bug:

- Describe the behavior accurately.
- Do not insult other users or contributors.
- Do not post personal or sensitive information.
- Do not encourage bypassing anti-cheat or modifying game binaries.

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when necessary to keep the project safe and useful.
